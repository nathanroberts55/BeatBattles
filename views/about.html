What is JamSession?

In the dynamic world of Twitch streaming, sharing music can be a risky business. Streamers and viewers alike often find themselves clicking on random links, with no guarantee of safety or quality. Enter JamSession, an innovative solution designed to make music sharing safe, centralized, and enjoyable.

Our app connects to various live streams, watching the chat for music links from popular sharing sites. These links are then transformed into an embeddable player, displayed on our website for everyone to enjoy. No more scrolling through chat history, no more clicking on random links. With JamSession, you can join the musical journey of your favorite streamer, safely and seamlessly.
